Return Policy

Our return policy allows returns within 30 days for unused items. Items must be in original packaging with tags attached.

To initiate a return:
1. Log in and go to My Orders.
2. Select the order and item you wish to return.
3. Choose a reason (defective, wrong item, changed mind, etc.).
4. We will send a prepaid label if the return is approved.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method or wallet.

Electronics and perishables may have different return windows; check the product page before purchase.
