# Shipping and Delivery

## Delivery times

Standard delivery is 3–7 business days after dispatch, depending on your location. We ship from warehouses in Bangalore, Delhi, and Mumbai.

Express delivery (1–2 business days) is available at checkout for eligible pincodes.

## Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also track from My Orders by entering your order number.

## International shipping

We currently ship only within India. International shipping may be introduced in the future.

## Damaged or missing items

If your delivery is damaged or items are missing, contact support within 48 hours with your order number and photos (if applicable). We will arrange a replacement or refund as per our return policy.
