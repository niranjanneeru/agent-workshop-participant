Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your order at no charge before it ships. Once the order has been shipped, cancellation is not possible; you may use our return process after delivery.

To cancel:
- Go to My Orders and select the order.
- Click Cancel order if the option is available (only for orders in "confirmed" or "processing" state).

If payment was already captured, we will refund the full amount within 5–7 business days.

For orders placed using a coupon or discount, the refund will reflect the amount paid after the promotion.
